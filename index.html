🏢 RAGHU BUILDERS

We design homes, schools, and buildings with passion and precision since 2017.


Founded in 2017, Raghu Builders is a dynamic architectural firm committed to designing inspiring and functional spaces. With a strong focus on residential homes, schools, commercial buildings, and institutional structures, we blend creativity with practicality to deliver designs that not only meet client expectations but also enhance the way people live, learn, and work. Our team of skilled architects and designers brings together innovation, sustainability, and technical precision to every project. Whether it’s a modern family home, a student-centered educational facility, or a community-focused commercial space, we tailor each design to reflect the unique needs and vision of our clients.


What We Offer:


👉 Custom home architecture and interior planning


👉 School and educational building design


👉 Commercial and institutional space planning


👉 3D visualization and project walkthroughs


👉 Sustainable and environment-friendly solutions
